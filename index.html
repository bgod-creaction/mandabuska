About the collective

mission
Mission (short)

We spread and strengthen Brazilian peripheral arts in Freiburg, focusing on music and other expressions like visual, plastic, and performing arts.

Mission (long)

The collective exists to showcase and amplify Brazilian peripheral arts, centered on music while embracing visual, plastic, and performing arts. We create spaces for making, exchange, and cultural appreciation, helping independent artists connect with the Freiburg scene.
